FireSMP

Windows Installation & Setup Guide

STEP 1

Download Modrinth

Go to this download: Modrinth Website

Click the "Download Modrinth App" button.

STEP 2

Download Start

You will see the download start in your browser.

STEP 3

Open Installer

Once the download finishes, open your downloads folder. Find the file named Modrinth App_0.10.23_x64-setup.exe.

Double-click it to open it.

STEP 4-5

Run Setup

A new window will open up with the Modrinth App Setup.

Click Next until you finish the setup, then click Finish.

STEP 6

Open App

The Modrinth app should now have automatically opened.

STEP 7-10

Account Sign-In

Look towards the right side; you should see a little Steve head icon.

Click it, and a sign-in button should appear. Click the green sign-in button.

A Minecraft Sign-in window will appear. Login to your account.

Once logged in, your account name and skin will show where the Steve icon was.

STEP 11-12

Create Instance

Click the plus icon found on the left side.

Select From File. Download the modpack using the button below:

Download FireSMP Modpack

Click the import from file button.

STEP 13-15

Importing

It will open an Explorer window. Go to your downloads folder and find Fire SMP 0.0.1.mrpack. Select it and click Open.

You'll see it download the pack in the top right. Once done, it will appear on the left side.

STEP 16-17

Launch Game

Click on the modpack, then click the green play button. It will turn red while starting.

STEP 21-22

Join FireSMP

If you see a "mods" button on the main menu, you're good! Go to multiplayer, add FireSMP, and join.

VOICE CHAT SETUP

Simple Voice Chat

Once in the server, press V to open the setup menu. Click Next.

AUDIO SETUP

Speakers & Microphone

Select your headphones for the speaker and your preferred microphone. I recommend System Default for both unless you have a specific setup.

ACTIVATION

Voice Activation

I recommend using Voice Activation so people can hear you whenever you talk without pressing a button. Click Next to finish.

FINAL STEP

Unmute (Press M)
