FireSMP

MacOS Installation & Setup Guide

STEP 1

Download Modrinth

Go to this download: Modrinth Website

Click the "Download Modrinth App" button.

STEP 2

Check Download Progress

You will see the download start in your browser.

STEP 3

Open the Installer

Once the download finishes, open your Downloads folder. Find the file named Modrinth App_0.10.23_universal.dmg.

Double-click it to open it.

STEP 4

Install Window

A new window will open up with the Modrinth App icon and an Applications icon.

STEP 5

Drag to Applications

Drag the Modrinth App icon into the Applications folder.

STEP 6

Launch Modrinth

Close the Modrinth App installation window and launch the Modrinth App from your Applications folder.

STEP 7

Security Warning

When you open the app, it will say: "Modrinth App is an app downloaded from the internet. Are you sure you want to open it?"

Click Open.

STEP 8

The Launcher

You will now see the Modrinth App open.

STEP 9-11

Account Sign-In

Look towards the right side; you should see a little Steve head icon.

Click it, and a sign-in button should appear. Click the green sign-in button.

A Minecraft Sign-in window will appear.

STEP 12

Logged In

Log in to your Minecraft account. Once logged in, you should see your account name and skin where the Steve head used to be.

STEP 13-14

Create Instance

Click the plus icon found on the left side.

It will pop up a menu that says "Creating an Instance." Select From File.

Download FireSMP Modpack

Click the import from file button.

STEP 15-18

Importing the Pack

It will open a Finder window. Go to your downloads folder and find the file named Fire SMP 0.0.1.mrpack.

Select it and click Open.

You'll see it start to download the FireSMP modpack in the top right corner.

Once it is done, you will see it listed on the left side.

STEP 19-21

Launch Minecraft

Click on the modpack, then click the green play button.

The play button will turn red while starting.

When you get to the main menu, if you see a "mods" button, the mods have been successfully added.

STEP 22-23

Join the Server

Go to multiplayer, add FireSMP, and join it.

When you join, a pop-up will appear asking if Modrinth can use your microphone.

Click Allow. Your mic will not work if you click "Don't Allow." If you accidentally clicked "Don't Allow," go to System Settings -> Privacy & Security -> Microphone and manually enable Modrinth App.
STEP 24-25

Voice Chat Setup

Once in the server, you'll see a chat message: Simple Voice Chat - Press V to set up.

Press V to open the setup menu. Click Next.

STEP 26

Select Speaker

Choose where you want the voice chat sound to come from (e.g., AirPods instead of MacBook speakers).

I recommend leaving it on System Default. Click Next.

STEP 27

Select Microphone

Select which microphone to use. I recommend using your MacBook Microphone if you want your AirPods to have the best sound quality, or your AirPods Microphone if you want to be heard more clearly.

If you can't decide, just use system default. Click Next.

STEP 28-29

Activation Method

Voice Activation: Mic is on whenever you talk.
Push to Talk: You must press a key for people to hear you.

I recommend Voice Activation. Click Next through the adjustments unless you know what you are doing.

STEP 30

Finish Setup

Read what it says on the screen, then click Finish Setup.

STEP 31

Unmute Yourself

You will start off muted. You can tell by the mute icon in the bottom left corner.

Press the M key on your keyboard to unmute (or mute again later).

STEP 32

Testing

To test if it works, talk! You should see a little mic icon pop up in the bottom left corner whenever you speak.

To adjust voice chat settings, press V at any time.
